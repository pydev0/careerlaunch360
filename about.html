Who We Are

Career Launch 360 is a leading career consultancy and IT training institute dedicated to transforming lives through education and career guidance. With years of experience in the industry, we've helped thousands of students and professionals achieve their dreams of studying abroad, landing their dream jobs, and building successful careers in technology.

Our team of experienced counselors, industry practitioners, and education experts work together to provide comprehensive support at every stage of your career journey. Whether you're a fresh graduate looking to break into tech, a professional seeking career advancement, or a student dreaming of studying at a top university abroad, we're here to guide you every step of the way.
