Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, services, and processes.

What courses do you offer? +
We offer comprehensive IT training programs including Full Stack Web Development, Machine Learning & AI, Data Science & Analytics, Cloud Computing, and more. All courses are designed with industry requirements in mind and include hands-on projects and placement support.
Do you provide placement assistance? +
Yes! We provide comprehensive placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, and direct connections with hiring companies. Our placement rate is 95% and we have partnerships with top tech companies.
Which countries do you help students apply to? +
We assist students with applications to universities in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Ireland, New Zealand, and other popular study destinations. Our counselors have expertise in various education systems and can guide you to the best options based on your profile.
What is the duration of your IT training programs? +
Course duration varies by program. Full Stack Development is 6 months, Machine Learning is 5 months, and Data Science is 4 months. All programs include flexible timings with both weekday and weekend batches available.
Do you offer online or offline classes? +
We offer both online and offline classes to suit your preferences. Our online classes are live and interactive with the same quality as offline sessions. You can choose the mode that works best for you.
What are the eligibility criteria for your courses? +
Most of our IT courses are open to anyone interested in learning tech skills, regardless of educational background. For some advanced courses like Machine Learning, basic programming knowledge is recommended. We assess each student's background during the counseling session.
How much does career counseling cost? +
We offer a free initial consultation session to understand your goals and recommend the best path forward. After that, our counseling packages are competitively priced based on the level of support you need. Contact us for detailed pricing information.
What is your refund policy? +
We have a transparent refund policy. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 15 days of enrollment (before 3 classes), you can request a full refund. After that period, refunds are processed on a case-by-case basis. Please refer to our terms and conditions for complete details.
Do you help with visa applications? +
Yes, we provide complete visa assistance including documentation preparation, application filing, interview preparation, and follow-up. Our team has extensive experience with visa processes for all major study destinations and has a high success rate.
Can I get a certificate after completing the course? +
Yes, upon successful completion of the course and all projects, you will receive an industry-recognized certificate from Career Launch 360. This certificate is valued by employers and demonstrates your proficiency in the relevant technologies.
